About Our
Organization

Education And Livelihood


We believe that education opens doors and livelihood sustains freedom. At Vishu Foundation, we ensure that individuals—especially children, youth, women, and the economically weaker sections—have the tools they need to learn, grow, and become self-reliant.

Plantation & Environmental Protection


Vishu Foundation believes protecting the environment is a shared responsibility. Through our green initiatives, we aim to build eco-aware communities by encouraging sustainable habits. Our efforts include tree plantations in urban, rural, and deforested areas, clean-up drives to manage waste and reduce plastic use, water conservation through rainwater harvesting, and school programs to teach environmental values from an early age.

Healthcare, Public Hygiene & Medical Relief

Access to basic healthcare and sanitation remains a challenge for many in India. Vishu Foundation is dedicated to bridging this gap by providing essential medical services and spreading awareness in underserved communities. Our initiatives include free medical camps, mobile health units for remote areas, blood donation and vaccination drives, first-aid training, clean water programs through filter distribution and water stations, and public toilet construction along with hygiene awareness campaigns.

Food Distribution


Hunger and malnutrition continue to affect countless individuals, especially in underprivileged communities. Vishu Foundation is committed to combating this issue by ensuring access to nutritious food for those in need. Our food distribution efforts include regular meal drives for the homeless and low-income families, ration kit distribution during emergencies and disasters, partnerships with local kitchens to serve cooked meals, and awareness programs on nutrition and food hygiene to promote long-term well-being.
